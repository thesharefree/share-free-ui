Share Free about

Share Free is a free to use platform where virtual support groups can be created and virtual circle meetups can be held.

  • Welcome to Share Free | Your Support Groups!

    Our mission is to create a safe and supportive space where individuals can connect and find the support they need. We believe that virtual support groups can provide an accessible and convenient way for people to connect with others who may be going through similar experiences.

    With Share Free, you can create your own virtual support group, or join an existing group based on your interests or needs. You have the option to keep your group private and invite only the people you want to join, or you can open it up to the wider community.

    Our app also includes features such as secure messaging and scheduling tools to make it easy for you to connect with your group members and stay organized.

    We understand that sharing personal information and experiences can be difficult, which is why we take your privacy and security seriously. We have implemented measures to protect your information and ensure that you have control over what you share within your virtual support group.

    At Share Free, we are committed to continuously improving and evolving our app to meet the needs of our users. We welcome your feedback and suggestions on how we can make our app even better.

    Thank you for choosing Share Free | Your Support Groups. We are here to support you.
