Privacy Policy

We at PayCr give our customers the highest priority. All the customer related data are respectively mapped and is only accessible to the intended user. All the practices and policies related to customerâ€™s privacy is shared in detail.

  1. Customer information and its use:
    The Policy to use customer information in a myriad of scenarios is shared below.
    1. Personal details (Create & Update)

      While registering a merchant we request for a unique Email id, unique mobile number. A password reset link will be sent to the new email for onboarding. The email ID and Mobile number will be used to communicate the transactional, promotional and policy level updates. Update on the user credentials can be done by the user through the portal via proper authentication and notification.

      We will use your personal details solely to render you the services and updates related to PayCr Services. We will not share the information to any third party for promotional purposes, nor will utilize it for any purposes not expressly consented to by you.

      Whenever you choose to refer a friend, we will use the details to send a one-time communication. We post user testimonials on our website. These testimonials may include customer information which we confirm from users prior to posting these on our website. PayCr is not responsible for the Personal Information users chooses to post within their posted testimonials.

    2. Customer Credentials and contents of user accounts

      PayCr stores and maintains file, documents and other user related credentials at a secondary secured location. This is done to prevent loss of data due to system or infrastructure failure.

    3. Online Payment

      PayCr sends payment links to your consumers for invoices on your behalf at your trigger only.

    4. Billing and Payment Information

      We do not store any details of your payment except of the following:

      1. Payment Reference No
      2. Payment Status
      3. Payment Mode

      We do not store any card information or preferences. We use third party services to process online payments.

    5. Device Details:

      The login device details are stored in the form of cache and cookies. This is done to enhance your experience.

  2. Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies:

    Technologies such as cookies, tags and scripts etc. are used to analyze customer activities, preferences, session and other demographic data to provide contextual services. Cookies can be deleted from your browser.

  3. External Website links:

    Users might find external website links on the PayCr website in the form of advertisements or references or links. Any activity post landing on the third party page will be dealt under the privacy guidelines of the third party. PayCr has no role to play in it.

  4. User Authentication:

    One can login using PayCr site using inhouse credentials. These credentials are stored in encrypted formats and are safe as per industry standards.

  5. Information Sharing:

    PayCr has the right to share user information with PayCr affiliates to inform you about related products. In case of merger and acquisition PayCr reserves the right to share the customer information for further use. Customers will be informed about the development and ensured that no misuse is done with the customer information.

  6. Data Security:

    Data Security is of prime concern to PayCr business and will employ standard protocols to secure the data. Cryptographic private and public key encryption is done to the passwords to ensure misuse of the platform even from a database administrator level.

  7. Notifications & Promotional Activities:

    Users will be sent notifications about the transaction through SMS and E-mail. They might receive email notifications for promotions, new products and updates. They will have the right to opt out of these services by clicking unsubscribe button in the email.

  8. Right to share information: Investigation of Illegal Activity

    Our employees will have the right to access to your personal details in case we suspect a illegal or fraudulent activity on the account. In any case we will ensure that the activity is not the violation of your rights and in line with the define privacy rules of the company.

  9. Enforcing the Privacy Policy:

    PayCr will periodically ensure that the privacy policies are implemented and practiced with utmost seriousness. Regular audit checks will be done at the organizational level to safeguard the privacy of the users.

  10. Notify Changes:

    Any change in the company policy, privacy policy or product guidelines impacting the consumer will be notified through E-mail to the user using the company Email ID.

  11. Reach us:

    In case of any concern related to privacy policy or product users can reach out to PayCr through care@paycr.in
