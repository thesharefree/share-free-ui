Share Free FAQs

Below are answers to the most asked questions

  1. What is Share Free?
    Share Free is a platform where virtual suppport groups can be created and virtual circle meetups can be scheduled. Users can:
    - create groups;
    - organize their groups in different houses;
    - host events, QnAs, guest lectures for your house;
    - add/remove members in your groups;
    - search and join groups on the basis of topics (alcoholism, depression, cancer,..);
    - etc

  2. How many maximum number of groups can a user create?

    Any user can create a maximum of 40 groups.

  3. How many Houses can a user create?

    Any user can create a maximum of 10 houses.

  4. How many maximum number of groups can a house have?

    Any house can have a maximum of 20 groups.

  5. How many maximum number of members can be in a group at a time?

    At any time, the total number of members in a group can not exceed 20.

  6. Can other users see your personal details like email, phone-no?

    No, your personal details are masked when someone access your profile.

  7. How can you reach us for queries or help?

    In case of any concern related to privacy policy or product users can reach out to contact@sharefree.co.in
